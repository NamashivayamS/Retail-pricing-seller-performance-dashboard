# 📊 Retail Pricing & Seller Performance Intelligence Dashboard

⭐ If you find this project useful, feel free to star the repository!

---

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze retail data across pricing, seller performance, and customer feedback.
It consolidates fragmented data into a unified analytical view, enabling better visibility into key business metrics and supporting data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze pricing strategies and their impact on revenue
* Evaluate seller performance across different segments
* Understand the relationship between customer ratings and sales
* Identify bottlenecks and opportunities for business process optimization

---

## 📊 Key Features

* Interactive dashboard with dynamic filters and drill-down capabilities
* KPI tracking for revenue, units sold, and customer ratings
* Seller-wise performance comparison and segmentation
* Pricing trend analysis across product categories
* Clean and structured data model for efficient querying

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified optimal mid-range pricing contributing to improved revenue trends
* Observed a positive correlation between customer ratings and sales performance
* Detected **20–30% variation** in performance across seller segments
* Highlighted areas for improving seller efficiency and pricing strategies

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI creation and calculations
* **Data Modeling** – Relationship building and schema design
